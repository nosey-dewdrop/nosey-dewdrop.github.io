privacy policy

last updated: march 27, 2026

overview

damla.log is a personal developer companion app. your privacy matters — the app is designed to keep your data on your devices.

data storage

all your tasks, ideas, issues, and versions are stored locally on your device and synced via your personal icloud account. data never leaves your icloud container. we do not operate any servers and have no access to your data.

github integration

if you choose to connect github, the app uses oauth device flow to obtain a read-only access token. this token is stored securely in your device's keychain. the app only reads your public repository names to use as tags. no repository content, code, or private data is accessed. you can disconnect at any time from settings.

data collection

damla.log does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. there are no analytics, no tracking, no third-party SDKs, no advertising.

third-party services

the only external services used are apple icloud (for sync between your devices) and github api (optional, for repo name tagging). both are subject to their own privacy policies.

children

the app is not directed at children under 13.

changes

if this policy changes, the updated version will be posted here.
