about me

I'm Damla Su Bilge — my name means drop in English. CS student at Bilkent University, President of IEEE Bilkent, and former TA for CS115 (Python). Before CS, I was studying medicine at Medipol — my family is full of doctors, but life had other plans after cancer treatment. I found computer science, and what started as an unexpected path became a real passion.

I started Python in 2022 while still in med. Now I work across Java, C++, JavaScript, and TypeScript. As a TA, I discovered students learn best by debugging — that insight became LadyBug. I don't believe in fighting AI with traditional coding pedagogy. Coding is evolving; imagination, project management, and critical evaluation of AI output are the new literacy.

I'm a bizarre person, and I consider that a virtue. I read Schopenhauer and play guitar. I study tarot and build APIs. Why so many projects? Because I have nothing to lose and everything to create. This lifetime is mine, and every project here is curiosity, stubbornness, and a belief that technology should serve connection and beauty. 🌻
